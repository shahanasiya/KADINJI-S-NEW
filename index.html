TOP THINGS TO DO

KADINGI'S ECOTOURISAM

Kadingi's ecotourism is one of the most popular tourist destination in kerala. Holiday on the traditional Kettuvallam, are an out-of-this-world experience. Later we visit kadalundi Bird sanctuary.

For Booking
ENJOY 12-HOUR RIVER HOLIDAY

Morning after breakfast leave for a wildlife viewing to the sanctuary on Lake kadalundi by boat . After two hours of mangroves viewing get back to the kadingi's home for lunch.

For Booking
KERALA CUSINE WITH SEA FOOD

From Fish Mappas to Fish Moilee and Prawns Chilly Fry to Grilled Seer Fish, Kerala cuisine has a lot to offer to fish lovers. We have picked some of thefinest fish delicacies with their recipes so that you can try making them at home in authentic Kerala style. These recipes have been compiled from households across the State and are wonderful representatives of Kerala cuisine.
